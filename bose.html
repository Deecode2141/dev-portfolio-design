Bose email design

The process

This email design project process outlines the comprehensive journey of promoting a Bose blue dust-resistant headset. By focusing on user research, responsive design, and compelling content, the campaign successfully communicates the product's benefits and drives engagement.

  • Design wireframes: For the email layout, ensuring an engaging and intuitive structure.

  • Example: I included sections for product images, a description of the products, and a clear call to action (CTA).

  • Challenge: Some images did not render correctly on mobile devices (like the hero image) during initial tests.

  • Solution: I Adjusted image sizes and optimize loading times to ensure compatibility across all devices.

Shiseido email design

The process

This email design project process outlines the journey of promoting Shiseido's age-in skincare products through a dynamic 3-day flash sale using AMP technology. By focusing on user research, creating a sense of urgency, and ensuring a fully responsive design, the campaign successfully drives engagement and sales.

  • Incorporated an AMP-powered countdown timer to introduce a dynamic element of urgency.

  • Example: I used AMP components to build a real-time countdown timer that updates dynamically as the sale progresses.

  • Challenge: Some email clients do not support AMP, potentially limiting reach.

  • Solution: I created a fallback version of the email that includes static content for non-AMP supported clients, ensuring all recipients can access the promotion.

Pizza email design

The process

This project outlines the process of designing a mobile-responsive email campaign for Raggio's Pizza Pie, focusing on creativity and problem-solving. The email design features a visually engaging GIF of slices being taken, employs color psychology to highlight the fresh ingredients in each pizza, and incorporates a marketing strategy that showcases the deliciousness of the product and drives customer engagement.
